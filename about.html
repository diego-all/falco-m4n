About Falco

Learn about Falco's implementation, security metrics, and how it helps mitigate risks within your organization.

What is Falco?

Falco is the open-source cloud-native runtime security project originally created by Sysdig. It monitors and detects anomalous activity in applications, containers, and infrastructure in real-time.

Implementation at Our Organization

At our organization, Falco is integrated as a cornerstone of our security posture. It is deployed across our Kubernetes clusters and integrated with CI/CD pipelines to ensure compliance and detect unauthorized behaviors.

Key Metrics

  • Detection Coverage: Over 95% of known threats detected in real-time.
  • False Positive Rate: Less than 5%, minimizing operational noise.
  • Automated Responses: 85% of incidents automatically mitigated through Falco rules.

Mitigated Security Risks

Falco helps mitigate various security risks, including:

  • Unauthorized container executions.
  • Privilege escalations within containers.
  • Access to sensitive files and configurations.
  • Suspicious network activities and process executions.

By integrating Falco, our organization has significantly reduced the likelihood of breaches and improved our incident response capabilities.
