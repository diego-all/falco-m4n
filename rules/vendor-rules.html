Vendor Rules

Falco comes with a comprehensive set of preconfigured rules for container, host, Kubernetes, and cloud security, with the option to customize your own rules. In the baseline defined for MELI, 38 out of the 75 default rules were integrated to identify risks and common bad practices in containerized environments.
